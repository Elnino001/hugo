HUGO BRIS ADVISORY Alternative & Open architecture investment solution.

Open architecture investment solution.

The Open Architecture Investment Solutions unit is a dedicated team within AUDRIC DARENSBOURG ADVISORY Asset Management. We take an open-architecture approach because it allows us to invest in the funds that most closely match our clients’ investment needs – even if these are not our funds. This gives us the freedom to focus purely on what our clients want. We offer innovative, bespoke solutions and customized portfolio management services. And we manage a diversified range of funds of funds including funds of hedge funds, long-only funds and combinations of both.

With a presence in Hungary , Dubai , New York, Paris, London and Zurich, our international management team provides access to the most talented alternative asset managers in the world.


Our expertise

We invest in three major families of alternative strategies:

Multi-Strategy
Trading Oriented
Long/short equity and quantitative strategies
Capital preservation is achieved through diversification across strategies and by carefully selecting the very best fund managers.

Our team has an excellent track record when it comes to selecting the best fund managers, because they are free from conflicts of interest and can focus solely on the specific interests of each client.


Our philosophy


Our fund portfolios are made up of high conviction investments, with well-diversified sources of both risk and return. We aim to capture as much performance upside as possible, while maintaining a strict focus on minimising downside risk.
